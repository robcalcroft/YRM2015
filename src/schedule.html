Schedule

All participants are invited to give a short (15 minutes + 5 minutes for questions) talk on their research. These talks will be organised into tracks which reflect different fields of research.

There will be plenary and keynote talks from distinguished speakers across mathematics, a poster competition, and a Quantitative Finance workshop from G-research. For the first 160 delegates to register, the registration fee includes a three course conference dinner at Somerville College, Oxford.

A full schedule will be posted nearer the time.

Update: attendees at the BMC/BAMC can have their registration fee refunded if they attend YRM2015. See details.
