Schedule

YRM2015 will be officially opened on Monday 17th August by Professor Sir Andrew Wiles.

During the conference, participants will give short (20 minutes + 5 minutes for questions) talk on their research. These talks will be organised into tracks which reflect different fields of research.

There will be plenary and keynote talks from distinguished speakers across mathematics, a poster competition, and a Quantitative Finance workshop from G-research. On the evening of the 18th there will be a public lecture co-organised with the London Mathematical Society.

There will also be a three course conference dinner at Somerville College, Oxford on the 19th (limited to 180 places due to the size of the dining hall). There will be wine receptions with assorted cold refreshments on the evenings of the 17th and 18th.

The full schedule is in the conference booklet shown below.
