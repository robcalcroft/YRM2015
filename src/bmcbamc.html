BMC/BAMC

We are grateful to the London Mathematical Society for funding 30 bursaries for PhD students attending both the 4th Joint British Mathematical Colloquium & British Applied Mathematics Colloquium (BMC/BAMC 2015) and YRM2015. These cover the cost of the registration fee for YRM2015.


If you want to claim a bursary, you must contact us as soon as possible. If your application is successful then, once you have attended both the BMC/BAMC 2015 and YRM, we will be able to refund your registration fee for YRM2015.
