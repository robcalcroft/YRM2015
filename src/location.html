Where are we?

YRM2015 will be held in the Andrew Wiles Building (Mathematical Institute at the University of Oxford). A limited number of discounted rooms are available for YRM attendees at Lincoln College, a 10 minute walk away in the centre of Oxford. The conference dinner will be held at Somerville College, next door to the Mathematical Institute.

We are a short walk from Oxford train and coach stations. See National Rail, National Express, or megabus for cheap advance coach tickets.

If you are travelling by car, we recommend using the Pear Tree Park and Ride and catching the bus route 300, which stops opposite the Mathematical Institute. Accessible parking places at the Institute are available by prior booking with a clear explanation of the circumstances of need.
