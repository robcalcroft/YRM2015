Where are we?


This year we are located in the heart of Oxford at the Made Up Centre. This wonderful venue will be the home for the conference this year. It's super easy to find. Follow the signs for the Made Up Centre when you exit the A4. Alternatively use the map below to plan directions from your current location.

The address is:

Made Up Centre, Somewhere in, Oxford, MAD4 UP.
